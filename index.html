About SIPMAN

SIPMAN was born from a simple belief — soda should never be ordinary. Inspired by the iconic Indian goli soda, we set out to revive a classic with a bold, modern twist.

We’ve worked tirelessly to discover and perfect exotic flavors that excite the palate and stand apart from the usual. Every SIPMAN bottle blends nostalgia, innovation, and attitude, delivering a refreshment that feels both familiar and new.

We don’t follow trends — we create flavors that make every sip memorable.
