Our Story

SIPMAN was born from a simple belief — soda should never be ordinary. Inspired by the iconic Indian goli soda, we set out to revive a classic with a bold, modern twist.

We’ve worked tirelessly to discover and perfect exotic flavors that excite the palate and stand apart from the usual.

We don’t follow trends — we create flavors that make every sip memorable.

Our Vision

SIPMAN represents a new generation of Indian beverages — confident, energetic, and unapologetically bold.

Our goal is to redefine refreshment while staying rooted in flavors people love and trust.
