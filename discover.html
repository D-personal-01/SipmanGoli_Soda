Our Story

SIPMAN was born from a simple idea — why should soda be ordinary? Inspired by the iconic Indian goli soda, we set out to build a beverage brand that celebrates bold taste and fearless identity.

Every SIPMAN bottle carries nostalgia, innovation, and attitude. We don’t follow trends — we create flavors that stand out.
